Glossary

Challenge period: A period of time that starts at the creation of a challenge and lasts one month. During this period jurors cannot vote.

Clencher: A clencher of a specific challenge is the user who started it.

Juror: A juror of a specific challenge is a user who got invited by the clencher of the challenge to vote on his/her performance at the end of the challenge period.

Voting period: A period of time that starts as soon as the challenge period ends and lasts one week. During this period Jurors can make their votes and provide feedback.
